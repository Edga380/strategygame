# Strategy game
Earth needs more resources to sustain mankind. After finding a planet with a lot of it they think their problems are solved. While building a new base on the planet and harvesting its resources they encounter a species that have been harvesting it too, so the fight for resources begins.
#
## Why I chose to develop this game?
A little bit of history. When I was a little kid I went to a friends place he had 'Sega genesis 2' and a game called 'Dune' I got hooked on it and ended up playing that game for hours that day.
Later on in my life I discovered that there is other versions of that game, the one I played the most and still sometimes play is called 'Emperor battle for dune'.
So after developing 'Bubble space wars' I started thinking about developing similar game to 'Dune' with unity but never had time to do it.
So when I had to develop a game for FAC I straigt away though about this game and that I really want to code it even though it's going to be really hard.

## How to play?
## 1. Mouse controls:
  ### Right mouse button:
     * Map movement
     * Cancel selected building.
     * Cancel fix building.
  ### Left mouse button:
     * Press buttons
     * When press, hold and drag rectangle is being drawn to select army units.
     * Selected army units can be moved by clicking anywhere on the map.
     * When clicked on enemy building selected army units will attack it.
## 2. Buildings:
  ### Main building:
  ![Main building](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/mainBuilding.png "Main building")
  
      * Without this building you can't build other buildings.
      * Limit - 1.
      * Hp - 200.
  ### Wall:
  ![Wall](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/wall.png "Wall")
  
      * Cost 500.
      * Blocks enemies.
      * Limit - 30.
      * Hp - 30.
  ### Solar panel:
  ![Solar panel](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/solarPanel.png "Solar panel")
  
      * Cost 800.
      * Supplies buildings with energy.
      * Limit - 10.
      * Hp - 50.
  ### Harvest depot:
  ![Harvesting building](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/harvestingBuilding.png "Harvesting building")
  
      * Cost 3000.
      * If there is enough energy you will be given harvesting vehicle too as a bonus.
      * Without this building harvesters have no where to empty out their harvest.
      * Limit - 1.
      * Hp - 100.
  ### Vehicle factory:
  ![Vehicle factory](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/vehicleFactory.png "Vehicle factory")
  
      * Cost 1200.
      * Enables to manufacture vehicles (Tank, Armored vehicle, Harvester).
      * Limit - 1.
      * Hp - 100.
  ### Soldier barracks:
  ![Soldier barracks](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/soldierBarracks.png "Soldier barracks")
  
      * Cost 1000.
      * Enables soldier training.
      * Limit - 1.
      * Hp - 100.

  ![Armored vehicle](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/armoredVehicle01.png "Armored vehicle")
  
      * Cost 1200.

      * Limit - 15.

  ### Harvester:
  ![Harvester](https://edga380.github.io/projectgallery/images/gallery1800x1800/harvester01.png "Harvester")
